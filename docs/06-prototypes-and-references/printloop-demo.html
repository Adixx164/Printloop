How it works

Three steps to your document

No queues. No USB drives. No awkward shop conversations.

Upload your document

Step 02

Features

Built for Nigeria's print economy

From NYSC corpers printing call-up letters to businesses printing contracts

Print from any device

Phone, laptop, or tablet — no drivers, no USB. Upload from WhatsApp, Google Drive, or email and we handle the rest.

Secure file handling

Files are encrypted in transit, stored on Cloudinary, and automatically deleted 24 hours after printing. Your documents stay private.

Paystack & wallet payments

Pay with card, bank transfer, or your PrintLoop wallet. Wallet top-ups are instant and balances never expire.

Group printing

Organise batch prints for an entire class or department. One host, multiple participants — one pickup token for the whole group.

Real-time tracking

Get push notifications when your job starts printing, when it's ready to collect, and when your document has been picked up.

Shop owner dashboard

Partner shops get a full operations panel — kiosk health, job queue, revenue analytics, and remote management tools.
